Introduction

I'm Armando Fernández. I have lived in three countries: Paraguay (my home country), Argentina, and Brazil. I was born in Asunción, Paraguay and lived there for 18 years. I served a mission in Argentina for two years, returned to Paraguay for one year, and later moved to Brazil where I currently live.

On this website I share my experiences living in these three countries — their cultures, foods, and people.
